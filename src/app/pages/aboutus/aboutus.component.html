September 22, 2021 08:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time

LOUISVILLE, Ky.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Yum! Brands, Inc. (NYSE: YUM) today announced the promotion
of Sabir Sami, 54, to KFC Division Chief Executive Officer, reporting to Yum! Brands Chief Executive
Officer David Gibbs, effective January 1, 2022. Sami, a 12-year veteran of the Company,
who currently serves as KFC Division Chief Operating Officer and Managing Director of KFC Asia,
will succeed Tony Lowings, who is stepping down as CEO at the end of 2021 in advance of his
retirement in early 2022. In this role, Sami will assume global responsibility for driving the
brand strategy and performance of KFC.
